Your attack scope is divided into the fifteen by sixteen grid of the ocean that the cloaked enemy fleet is known to inhabit. Shooting into a square on the grid destroys whatever part of a ship is in that square. Hit the ship in each square it inhabits and it sinks. Budgets are tight and you are limited to 25 shots. But the Admiral will allocate you two more shots every time you hit an enemy ship. Run out of shots before the fleet is sunk and the game is over.

Position your sights over a square of ocean with the Directional Arrows or joystick. Press the "A" button and a shot is fired into that square. Hitting a ship results in an explosion and the Game Message area (bottom left) confirms the "Hit!". Sinking a ship is also confirmed ("You sank my Battleship!").

Misses result in the ocean in that square turning darker blue and the Game Message area reports a "Miss!".

Keep track of the amount of shots you have left in the Game Statistics area (bottom right). The statistics show you the number of shots you have left (S:20); the number of hits (H:5); the number of missed shots (M:5); and the number of ships sunk — kills — (K:1).

Sinking all ten ships before you run out of shots wins the game! If you run out of shots before you sink all ten ships you lose — just give it another try. To quit the game, press the "X" button.
