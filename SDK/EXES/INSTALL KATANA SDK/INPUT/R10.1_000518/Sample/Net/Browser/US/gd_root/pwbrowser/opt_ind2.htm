Learning to Get Around

Very Good! Now a few navigation basics. To skip this part and get going to the Internet, select the "Step One" option below.

You can move the cursor using the D-pad or thumbpad. If you move the pointer to the edge of the screen, the screen will scroll in the direction that the cursor is moving.
When the cursor is over a link, the cursor will change. To click on it, press the A button.
After clicking, the cursor may change into "Busy" mode. The Browser becomes busy, and you need to wait until it has finished, when the cursor will return to normal.

Later, you will be asked to fill in some forms. Place the cursor over the entry box. Then press the A button. A purple block will appear in the box. Type in the information. When you are finished typing, press START button on your controller or CLOSE. You can also press TAB to move to the next field.

There is a special kind of Web page called a "Frames" page that is actually several web pages diplayed at once.

To scroll a specific Frame of a Frames page, move the cursor over a blank area in the Frame you are interested in, and press the A button.

To enter a web address (an URL) press the A button while the cursor is over the bar on the top of the screen.

Another helpful feature of the PlanetWeb Browser is the Magnifier. The Magnifier allows you to magnify the appearance of any object on the screen. This is helpful in reading small text or getting a close-up on a photo. Press the Y button. To enlarge the Magnifier, press the Y button again. To turn the Magnifier off, press the Y button one more time.
