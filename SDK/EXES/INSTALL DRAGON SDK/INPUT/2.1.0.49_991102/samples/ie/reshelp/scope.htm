The ships are required to be horizontal or vertical. They cannot be diagonal. They also cannot turn corners.

When a ship sinks, it's cloaking device is deactivated so you can tell what class of ship it was. Use this information to determine the remaining ship's lengths.

There are 240 (15x16) squares of ocean to search. There are 30 squares of ships to hit. Hitting a ship is a 1 in 8 chance. However, once you have made the first hit you know that there are only four squares possible where the next section of the ship could be located. Any previous hits and misses may limit the direction of the rest of the ship can be headed.
