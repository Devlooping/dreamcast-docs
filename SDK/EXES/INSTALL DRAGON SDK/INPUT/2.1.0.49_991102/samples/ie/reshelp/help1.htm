Intelligence reports have located an enemy fleet in the ocean in your territory. Unfortunately, the enemy fleet has a cloaking technology that makes the ships invisible. You are going to have to search for the fleet by combing the known area in a gird-like fashion and firing until you hit a ship. The goal of Battleship is to sink all of the ships in the enemy fleet before you run out of shots. The Intelligence reports also tell you the number of ships, their class, and their size.


Object of the Game
The fleet is made of the following ships:
Four Submarines
Three Destroyers
Two Cruisers
One Battleship
